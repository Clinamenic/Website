> Loneliness is about lacking the agency to form the relationships that we want.

This framing of loneliness is interesting because it centers agency, and it frames relational richness as the solution. Being lonely in this sense seems to involve an atrophy or even absence of relational faculties, which means the person in question likely has difficulty relating to others. Given a status quo of widespread loneliness, it makes sense why people feel so isolated and alienated, without even knowing how to make sense of the condition, yet alone a cure.
