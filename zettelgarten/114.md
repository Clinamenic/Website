> This is why loneliness also impacts people with busy social lives: If we are surrounded by relationships but those relationships obligate us to spend time in ways that we don't want to and there's nothing we can do about it, then we can feel just as lonely as someone isolated in their home.

This sheds a light on how people can be socially active but still have their relational needs go unmet, owing to their lack of relational intentionality.
