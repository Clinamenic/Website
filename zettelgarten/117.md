> As I strategized about how to fill my calendar with meaningful shared time, I began to conceive of a relationship as a **shared understanding that two or more people have of how to spend meaningful time together.** My friend Karuna and I weren't just holding two ends of a stick, we had a shared understanding that if we went to karaoke on Tuesday nights, it would make both of us happy.
