>  The fact that so many of us feel so lonely, so lacking in relational agency, is tied to the fact that we do not know how to find and cocreate the places where the relationships we need will manifest. Creating relational containers is work that the world needs, and the link between relationality and evolutionary rate provides us with a theoretical foundation for going about that work.
